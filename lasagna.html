Lasagna

Description

Delicious and hearty old fashioned lasagna recipe. Full of meat, sauce and cheese-the type of lasagna you hope that there's more of

Ingredients

For the Sauce

    1 tablespoon butter 2 lbs lean or extra lean ground beef 1 medium finely chopped onion 1 can Hunt's tomato sauce 10 fresh white mushrooms finely sliced 2 fresh garlic cloves finely cut 1 teaspoon dried oregano flakes .25 cup water if necessary

For the Filler Layer

    1 egg 1 container ricotta cheese 4 ounces of fresh spinach .5 cup parmesan cheese

The Noodles

    12 lasagna noodles

For the Layers

    1.5 cups cheddar cheese grated 1.5 cups mozzarella cheese grated Fresh parsley for garnish
