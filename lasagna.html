Lasagna

Description

Delicious and hearty old fashioned lasagna recipe. Full of meat, sauce and cheese-the type of lasagna you hope that there's more of

Ingredients

For the Sauce

    1 tablespoon butter 2 lbs lean or extra lean ground beef 1 medium finely chopped onion 1 can Hunt's tomato sauce 10 fresh white mushrooms finely sliced 2 fresh garlic cloves finely cut 1 teaspoon dried oregano flakes .25 cup water if necessary

For the Filler Layer

    1 egg 1 container ricotta cheese 4 ounces of fresh spinach .5 cup parmesan cheese

The Noodles

    12 lasagna noodles

For the Layers

    1.5 cups cheddar cheese grated 1.5 cups mozzarella cheese grated Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

The sauce

    1. Saute the onions and garlic in the butter 2. Add the mushrooms and cook for about 5 minutes 3. Add the ground beef and cook until there is no pink 4. Add all the wet sauce ingredients and let simmer 15 minutes

The Filler

    Mix all the filler ingredients together in a bowl and set aside

The Noodles

    1. Bring large pot of water to boil 2. Drop in the lasagna noodles and cook until soft but not mushy 3. Drain the water and then replace with cold water so the noodles can cool for handling. Leave the noodles sitting in the cold water

Assembly Instructions

Arrange in a lasagna pan

    1. Bottom layer-The meat sauce 2. Next layer- 4 cooked lasagna noodles 3. Next Layer- Cottage cheese and spinach layer and sprink the parmesan cheese on this layer 4. Next Layer- 4 cooked lasagna noodles 5. Top Layer- Put the rest of the grated cheese on the top layer

Baking Instructions

    Cover the pan with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Then remove foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is lightly brown and bubbly
